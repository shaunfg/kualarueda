Curriculum

Here's what we cover across beginner and intermediate levels.

01

Casino Fundamentals

Cuban motion, son clave timing, basic steps (Guapea, Dile Que No), and partner connection.

02

Turn Patterns & Partnerwork

Enchufla, Vacila, Setenta and their variations. Leading and following with clarity and ease.

03

Rueda de Casino

Everyone dances in a circle and follows calls from one person. Good for building vocabulary fast and dancing with the whole group.

04

Footwork & Styling

Footwork variations, body movement, and making the dance your own. Not required but worth exploring once you have the basics.

05

Cuban Music & Culture

A bit of context on son, timba, and salsa — what you're actually listening to and how that shapes how you move.

06

Social Dancing

Dancing with people you've never met, adjusting to different spaces, and knowing when to keep it simple.
